About Helios Fit

Adam Johnson, one of the most popular trainers over the last 7 years at Overtime Fitness (recognized as Best in Mountain View by The Voice for many consecutive years), started Helios Fit in January 2018. He is trusted by clients from Google, Microsoft, Ernst & Yang, and many others in the neighborhood. Born in a family of competitive fitness enthusiasts, he received his own training since early childhood, and has never stopped in perfecting his skills in fitness and techniques of personal training.
